Who We Are

About Monike Lighting

Monike Lighting is a design-driven lighting factory based in Guzhen, Zhongshan — the lighting capital of China. We develop and manufacture pendant, wall, linear and table fixtures for restaurants, hospitality and refined living spaces worldwide.

With design, tooling, production and quality control all handled in-house, we support global OEM and ODM customers from a reference image to a container of certified, branded product — with low MOQs, honest lead times and one English-speaking account manager per client.
