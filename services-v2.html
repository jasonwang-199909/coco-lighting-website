FAQs

Common Questions

What files do you need for a quotation?

A reference photo, drawing, or spec sheet is enough to start. For custom projects, dimensions and target price help us quote accurately within one business day.

What is your MOQ for OEM orders?

Standard models start at 20 pieces, and models can be mixed in one container. Custom OEM projects are typically 50–100 pieces depending on tooling.

How long does sampling take?

Pre-production samples ship in 7–15 days. Bulk production runs 15–25 days after sample approval, depending on quantity and finish.

Can you match my brand's finish and packaging?

Yes — any RAL color, laser-engraved logos, branded boxes, barcodes and multilingual manuals are all handled in-house.
