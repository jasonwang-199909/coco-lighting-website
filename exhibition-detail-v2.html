Why Visit Our Booth

The autumn edition is where we launch the year's most important collections. This October we are bringing the complete 2027 preview — including new Halo Ring sizes, the Linea DALI-2 office system, and an expanded rattan collection for resort projects.

  • Handle real production samples — every fixture on display is factory-built, not a prototype
  • Meet the export team who will actually manage your orders
  • Same-week factory visits to Guzhen can be arranged from the booth
  • Fair-exclusive trade pricing for orders confirmed within 30 days
