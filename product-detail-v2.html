Product Description

The premium Halo Ring Chandelier LM100 represents the flagship of our suspension collection, specifically engineered for lighting importers, hospitality project buyers and premium retail distributors. Featuring a seamless one-piece aluminium ring with fully recessed LEDs, this modern LED ring chandelier delivers an even, glare-free wash of warm light over dining tables and lobby voids. Designed and manufactured entirely in-house in Guzhen, it combines architectural presence with factory-direct pricing.

Seamless One-Piece Aluminium Ring with Premium Finish

The 90cm ring is extruded and bent from a single piece of aluminium, then welded and hand-polished until the seam disappears completely. The powder-coated and anodised surface resists fingerprints, oxidation and humidity during international shipping, keeping every batch showroom-ready from our warehouse to your customer's ceiling. Choose from Gold, Matte Black, Ivory or any custom RAL color to match your product line.

Glare-Free Dual-Optic LED System · 2700–4000K · CRI>90

High-density LED strips sit deep inside the profile behind a CNC-cut opal acrylic diffuser — zero visible diodes from any seated angle. Specify color temperature from 2700K to 4000K at order, with Triac, 0–10V or DALI dimming down to 5%. The 45W light source covers 10–15 m² of dining or lobby space with a CRI above 90, flattering both food and faces — the standard restaurants demand.

Machined In-House for Batch-to-Batch Consistency

Body machining, powder-coating, anodising and assembly all happen on our own six production lines in Guzhen, Zhongshan. The diffuser is CNC-cut to a 0.2mm tolerance, so light distribution stays identical across every unit — order 20 pieces or 2,000, they match. Every fixture passes our 12-checkpoint pre-shipment test including hi-pot, aging and photometric inspection.

Retail-Ready Export Packaging for International Logistics

Each chandelier ships in a double-wall carton with moulded foam, pre-wired canopy and illustrated installation guide — drop-tested to survive sea and air freight without finish damage. Private-label boxes, barcodes and multilingual manuals are available for retail and distribution partners, saving importers valuable prep and handling time.

Full OEM/ODM Customization for Your Brand

As a factory-direct OEM/ODM manufacturer, we customize diameter, drop height, finish, light source and packaging to your specification. Laser-engraved logos, custom RAL colors and fully private-label documentation mean your customers never see our name. MOQ starts at just 20 pieces with mixed-container support — samples ship in 7–15 days, bulk orders in 15–25 days, backed by CE, RoHS, SAA and GPSR (EU Rep) documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the diameter and drop height be customized?

Yes — the ring is produced in-house, so diameters from Ø60cm to Ø160cm and any drop height are available. Custom sizes add 3–5 days to sampling.

Which dimming systems are supported?

Triac (leading/trailing edge), 0–10V and DALI drivers are all available. Specify the protocol per market at order — EU, UK, US and AU driver variants are stocked.

What is the MOQ and lead time for this model?

MOQ is 20 pieces, and the LM100 can be mixed with other models in one container. Samples ship in 7–15 days; bulk production runs 15–25 days after approval.

Do you provide project documentation?

Yes — photometric files (IES), CE/RoHS/SAA certificates and GPSR documentation ship with every order, ready for specifiers and customs.

Can you laser-engrave our logo on the fixture?

Yes — logos are laser-engraved on the canopy or ring on our in-house line, with branded packaging, barcodes and manuals available for full private-label supply.
