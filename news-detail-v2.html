Monike at the Hong Kong International Lighting Fair 2026

Home  /  News  /  HK Lighting Fair 2026
20 May 2026Company News

This spring's Hong Kong International Lighting Fair was our strongest edition yet. Across four days at the HKCEC, our booth welcomed buyers from 38 countries and presented more than 40 new designs — including the first public showing of the Halo Ring series in its new ivory finish.

What Drew the Most Inquiries

Three collections dominated conversations at the booth: the Halo Ring chandeliers for hospitality projects, the Linea architectural system with its new DALI-2 drivers, and the rattan dome series that resonated strongly with buyers serving resort and coastal markets.

  • Halo Ring series — 120+ project inquiries, led by EU and Middle East buyers
  • Linea DALI-2 — strong interest from office fit-out contractors
  • Rattan Dome — resort buyers from Southeast Asia and Australia

See You at the Canton Fair

Next stop: Canton Fair Phase 1 (31 October – 4 November), where we will preview the 2027 collection alongside our full export line-up. Booth details will be announced in September — request an invitation below and we will send hall and booth numbers as soon as they are confirmed.
